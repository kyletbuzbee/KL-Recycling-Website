56 Years of Innovation

From a single truck in 1956 to 9 locations across Texas and Kansas. Click through our interactive timeline.

1956

Foundation & Beginning

Started with a single truck and a vision to serve East Texas businesses with honest, reliable scrap metal recycling.

1975

Expansion & Growth

Aquired additional facilities and expanded service area to include construction and industrial clients.

1995

Technology & Efficiency

Invested in modern processing equipment and computerized weighing systems for better accuracy and speed.

2010

Kansas Expansion

Opened first Kansas location and invested in mobile crushing and demolition services across both states.

2020

Digital Transformation

Launched online scheduling, real-time pricing, and mobile app for customer convenience.

2025

AI-Powered Future

Pioneering AI-based market analysis, environmental impact tracking, and automated pricing systems.
