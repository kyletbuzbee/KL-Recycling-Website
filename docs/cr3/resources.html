Why Recycle Metal?

Environmental Benefits

Metal recycling preserves natural resources, reduces energy consumption, and minimizes landfill waste. Recycling one ton of steel can save up to 2,500 pounds of iron ore.

Economic Value

Scrap metal has significant economic value. The global metal recycling industry contributes billions to the economy annually.

Community Impact

Supporting local recycling businesses helps create jobs and keep money circulating within our community.
