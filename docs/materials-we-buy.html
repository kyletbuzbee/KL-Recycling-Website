Materials We Buy

We accept a wide variety of ferrous and non-ferrous scrap metals. All materials are purchased for fair market value and weighed on certified scales.

Ferrous Metals (Magnetic)

Steel Beams & Structural

Structural steel beams, I-beams, rebar, and construction steel. Clean carbon steel preferred.

Preparation: Clean, no coated/painted materials
Examples: Beams, rebar, construction materials

Steel Cable & Wire

Steel cable, wire rope, and metal mesh used in construction and industrial applications.

Preparation: Clean and separated
Examples: Chain, wire rope, reinforcement mesh

Vehicles & Auto Parts

Cars, trucks, and large vehicle scrap. Tires and fluids must be removed.

Preparation: Drained of fluids, tires removed
Examples: Cars, trucks, buses, heavy equipment

Tin Cans & Containers

Steel tin cans, food containers, and beverage containers made from steel.

Preparation: Loose or crushed acceptable
Examples: Food cans, beverage containers

Construction Scrap

Mixed construction metals, rebar, wire mesh, and structural steel from demolition.

Preparation: Pre-sorted preferred
Examples: Demolition materials, construction waste

Steel Fasteners

Steel fasteners, clips, nuts, bolts, and small steel hardware items.

Preparation: Clean and dry
Examples: Nuts, bolts, clips, hardware

Non-Ferrous Metals (Non-Magnetic)

Copper Wire & Cable

Insulated and bare copper wire, electrical cables, and wiring from all sources.

Preparation: Clean bright wire preferred
Examples: Romex, THHN, coaxial, power cords

Copper Pipe & Plumbing

Copper plumbing pipes, fittings, and tubing from residential and commercial applications.

Preparation: Clean and separated
Examples: Pipe sections, fittings, plumbing fixtures
🥤

Aluminum Cans

Aluminum beverage cans and containers. Both crushed and loose acceptable.

Preparation: Labels not required
Examples: Soda cans, beer cans, aluminum containers

Aluminum Cast & Wheels

Cast aluminum parts, automotive wheels, transmission housings, and engine blocks.

Preparation: Separated from other materials
Examples: Car wheels, engine blocks, cast parts

Brass & Bronze

Plumbing fixtures, decorative brass, bronze fittings, and brass components.

Preparation: Yellow brass preferred
Examples: Faucets, door knobs, pipe fittings, fixtures

Stainless Steel

Stainless steel sinks, appliances, utensils, and industrial components.

Preparation: 300/400 series separated
Examples: Sinks, appliances, utensils, industrial parts

Equipment & Appliances

Electric Motors

Electric motors, transformers, generators, and electrical components.

Preparation: Drained, no fluids
Value: Copper windings recovered

Air Conditioners

Window and central AC units. Refrigerant must be certified removed.

Preparation: EPA-certified refrigerant removal
Note: Proper disposal required

Computers & Electronics

Desktop computers, laptops, circuit boards, and precious metal recovery from electronics.

Preparation: Data destruction recommended
Value: Gold, silver, copper content

Oilfield Equipment

Heavy industrial equipment, bulk crude tanks, drilling components, and oilfield machinery.

Preparation: Cleaned and decontaminated
Specialty: 400 bbl tanks and up
🏗️

Demolition Materials

Mixed construction metals from demolition projects with proper handling of hazardous materials.

Preparation: EPA-compliant disposal
Value: 90% recovery rate typical

Industrial Scrap

Manufacturing scrap, industrial equipment, and facility cleanout materials.

Preparation: Sorted and prepared
Volume: Large quantities welcome
