Understanding Your Metals

Learn about different metal types, current market values, and how we ensure fair pricing for all materials.

Ferrous Metals

Magnetic metals with iron content including steel, iron, and metal alloys used in construction and manufacturing.

Average: 60% of our volume

Examples: Steel beams, iron pipes, automotive scrap, construction materials. These metals are magnetic and form the foundation of our recycling business.

Non-Ferrous Metals

Valuable metals without iron content including copper, aluminum, brass, and stainless steel for higher market value.

Average: 30% of our volume

Examples: Copper wire, aluminum cans, brass fixtures, stainless steel sinks. These metals command premium prices due to their corrosion resistance and conductivity.

Precious Metals

High-value specialty commodities including gold, silver, platinum, and other rare metals from electronics and industry.

Average: 10% of our volume

Examples: Gold jewelry, silver teeth, platinum from catalytic converters, palladium from electronics. These specialty materials require expert handling and fetch the highest market prices.
